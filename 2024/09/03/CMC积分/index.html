\[\int_0^{\pi}f(sinx)dx=2\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}f(sinx)dx=2\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}f(cosx)dx\]

\[\int_0^{\pi}f(cos)dx=\left\{\begin{array}{**lr**}2\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}f(cosx)dx & &f偶\\0 & &f奇\end{array}\right.\]
